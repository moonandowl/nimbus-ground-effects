Technology

Fog That Stays Low. Technology That Stays Hidden.

Our systems use a specially engineered chiller and buried manifold network to produce a dense, ground-hugging fog layer that rolls across your landscape like a natural morning mist. The entire system is invisible — buried lines, concealed nozzles, and a compact control unit tucked out of sight.

About

We Make Fog. Really, Really Good Fog.

Nimbus Ground Effects was founded in 2019 by Marcus Bellweather, a mechanical systems engineer who spent over a decade designing atmospheric effects for theme parks and public attractions. After building fog systems for three major entertainment venues and two botanical garden installations, Marcus saw an opportunity to bring the same technology to residential and boutique commercial clients.

What started as a one-person operation in a garage in Argyle, Texas, has grown into a full-service fog engineering company with a team of experienced technicians, over a hundred successful installations, and a reputation for doing things right.

Our Approach

We don’t cut corners. Every Nimbus system is built from commercial-grade components, installed by trained technicians, and backed by real warranties. We’re not a landscaping company that added fog to the menu — we’re fog specialists who understand the engineering, the chemistry, and the craft.
